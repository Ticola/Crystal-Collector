Crystal Collector

  • 1) You will be given a random number at the start of the game: "Your Target"
  • 2) There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • 3) You win the game by matching "Your Score" to "Your Target" score. You lose the game if your score goes above the target score.
  • 4) The value of each crystal is hiden from you until you click on it.
  • 5) Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
